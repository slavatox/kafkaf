About informatics departament



    1990 – creation of departament of Informatics on the basis of ВТ УАИ;

    1998 – the first protection of the master's thesis on chair;

    1999 – first enrollment of students in the specialty 230301 – «Simulation and research of operations in organizational and technical systems»;

    2000-2002 – graduate student of departament Pavlov I.V. within operation over the master's thesis I developed the world famous archiver 7-zip (http://www.7-zip.org);

    2003 – transition to study of technology of visual object-oriented event programming within discipline «Informatics»;

    2004 – transition to examination in discipline of "Informatics" in the form of computer testing;

    • first release of engineers in the specialty 230301 – «Modelling and research of operations in organizational-technical systems»;

    2007 – defence of candidate's dissertation of one of the first graduating students of department on speciality 230301 «A design and analysis of operations are in the organizationally-technical systems» – Margamova A.V.;

    2007-2008 – participating of department in the Innovative educational program of university within the framework of the National project "Education" – segment of УНИЦ «Planning and exploitation of optical intersatellite communications» (project of development and launch satellite «УГАТУСАТ»);

    2009 – first reception of магистрантов on a department;

    • defence of two doctoral dissertations an associate professor Zozulya U.I. and by associate professor Tarhov S.V.;

    • an international conference is conducted "Free flight" of memory of Kabal'nov U.S.;

    2011 – the specialty 220402 «Special organizational and technical systems» is open, being the successor of specialty МС;

    2012 – the first All-Russian competition of final qualification operations in the specialty 230301 is organized and held – «Simulation and research of operations in organizational and technical systems»;

    • the first set of bachelors in the direction 230700 «Application informatics» on a profile «Application informatics in the information sphere».
